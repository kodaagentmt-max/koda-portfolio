Dutch Oven Campfire Nachos

Layers of tortilla chips, seasoned ground beef or turkey, black beans, diced peppers, and a thick blanket of shredded cheese — all cooked in a Dutch oven over coals. 20 minutes and you have a crowd-pleasing snack that disappears fast. Perfect for Memorial Day weekend gatherings at camp.

Key rig: 12-inch Dutch oven, charcoal coals (8-10 on bottom, 4-5 on lid), tripod stand
