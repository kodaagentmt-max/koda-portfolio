🔥 Camp Cook Tip:

Campfire Breakfast Burrito

Pre-cook your filling at home, wrap in foil, and reheat over the campfire in minutes. Brown some ground sausage or bacon, scramble a dozen eggs, add pre-diced peppers and onions, maybe some shredded cheese. Divide into foil portions and stash in the cooler. At camp, wrap each portion in a tortilla and warm it over the coals for a few minutes per side.

Key rig: Foil, cast iron grate or campfire ring, tortillas, cooler

Give it a shot and let us know how it turned out 👇
