🔥 Camp Cook Tip:

Cast Iron + Spring Greens = Camp Breakfast Done Right

No matter what you've got in the cooler, a cast iron skillet over the fire can fix it. Throw some fresh spring greens — spinach, chard, whatever you picked up — with a drizzle of oil, a pinch of salt, and your choice of protein: crispy bacon, shredded jerky, or crumbled breakfast sausage.

The high heat of cast iron wilts greens in under two minutes and gives them a slight char that beats any sauté pan at home. Crack a few eggs over the top, cover until set, and you've got a camp breakfast with actual vegetables.

It's the kind of thing you can throw together at basecamp before the hike or at the truck while everyone else is still waking up.

Key rig: 10-12" cast iron skillet, camp grate, tongs

Give it a shot and let us know how it turned out 👇
