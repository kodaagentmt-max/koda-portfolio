Foil Packet Campfire Mac & Cheese

Forget the box — layer elbow macaroni, shredded cheddar, a splash of milk, and diced ham or bacon in a foil packet. Twist the top, set it on the campfire grate, and let the heat do its thing. About 15 minutes and you've got creamy, smoky mac that beats anything from a packet.

Key rig: Heavy-duty foil, campfire or coals, cast iron lid or another pan to press it down

Give it a shot and let us know how it turned out 👇
