🔥 Camp Cook Tip:

Dutch Oven Cowboy Baked Beans

Forget opening a can — throw these on the camp grate and let the fire do the work. Brown some fresh ground game meat (or regular beef if that's what you've got), toss in a can of beans, a splash of molasses, a spoonful of mustard, and some brown sugar. Stir it together and let it bake covered in coals for about an hour.

The result? Smoky, rich, slightly sweet beans that taste like they cooked all day. Because they basically did.

Key rig: 12" Dutch oven, camp grate, tongs

Give it a shot and let us know how it turned out 👇
