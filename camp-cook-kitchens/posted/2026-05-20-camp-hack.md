🏕️ Camp Kitchen Hack

Use a cast iron skillet as a lid for your Dutch oven — it holds heat evenly and creates a perfect makeshift oven for baking biscuits or cornbread over coals.

Just place it face-down on top, add a few hot coals on the skillet, and rotate it once halfway through. No extra equipment needed.
