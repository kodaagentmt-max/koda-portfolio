Campfire Foil Packet Meals — Season Them Before the Trip

One of the easiest ways to level up your camp cooking: season your foil packet ingredients at home before you leave. Toss diced potatoes, seasoned protein, and veggies in a zip bag with olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper, and any herbs you've got. Pop them in the cooler pre-marinated. At camp, just tear open the bag and wrap it all up in a double layer of heavy-duty foil.

No measuring, no mess, no forgetting the seasoning at 6 AM by the fire.

Key rig: Heavy-duty foil, zip bags for prep, cast iron grate or coals
