🔥 Camp Cook Tip:

No-Cook Summer Camp Snacks

When it's hot and you don't want to fire up the stove, stock your cooler with ready-to-eat camp snacks. Think: trail cheese (the hard kind that handles warmth), summer sausage, crackers, nuts, dried fruit, and pre-cut veggies. Prep everything at home so at camp you're just opening the cooler.

This keeps energy up without heating up the kitchen — or yourself.

Key rig: Good cooler, hard cheese, summer sausage, crackers, pre-bagged nuts

Give it a shot and let us know how it turned out 👇
