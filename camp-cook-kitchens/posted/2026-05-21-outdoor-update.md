🏕️ Outdoor Update

Walleye season is heating up across the Upper Midwest — many state fisheries agencies are reporting strong spawn completion rates on Lakes Sakakawea and Oahe, setting up a solid summer bite for anglers in North Dakota and South Dakota.

The National Wild Turkey Federation's 2026 Grand National Turkey Competition wrapped results in late April, celebrating top harvests and habitat contributions from hunters across 42 states — a reminder that conservation dollars from license sales go straight back to the birds.

Spring crappie fishing continues to produce big numbers in oxbow lakes and reservoirs throughout the Missouri River basin. Pro anglers are reporting consistent catches in the 12–14 inch range, with minnows and soft plastics leading the bite.
