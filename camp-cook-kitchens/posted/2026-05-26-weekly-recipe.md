Cedar Plank Campfire Fish

If you're fishing this time of year, try cooking your catch on a soaked cedar plank over the campfire. The plank steams the fish from below while the open flame adds a light smoky char. Walleye, bass, trout — doesn't matter. A few herbs, lemon, and salt is all you need.

Key rig: Cedar plank (soaked 1-2 hours), campfire or coals, fish of your choice

Give it a shot and let us know how it turned out 👇
