Elk Shawarma Plate

🔥 Camp Cook Kitchen — Tag Soup • May 1, 2026

Elk shoulder marinated in a blend of cumin, coriander, cardamom, and lemon zest, then slow-roasted until the exterior crisps and the inside stays medium-rare. Slice thin and serve over warm pita with tahini sauce, pickled turnips, and fresh tomatoes.

Field Notes

Slow-roasted elk shoulder with shawarma spices and tahini sauce.
