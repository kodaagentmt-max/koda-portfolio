Venison Fried Pickles with Sriracha Aioli

🔥 Camp Cook Kitchen — Tag Soup • May 1, 2026

Thick-cut venison fat pickles breaded in seasoned cornmeal, fried golden at 375°F until they pop when you bite them. Serve with a cooling sriracha aioli. This is bar food from another level.

Field Notes
