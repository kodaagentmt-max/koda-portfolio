Duck Confit Tacos with Pickled Onions

🔥 Camp Cook Kitchen — Tag Soup • May 28, 2026

Duck leg confit shredded into crispy shreds, piled into warm corn tortillas with quick-pickled red onions, cotija cheese, and a squeeze of lime. The richness of the duck fat balanced against the bright acid of the pickles makes these tacos unforgettable.

Field Notes

Duck leg confit in warm corn tortillas with pickled onions and cotija. Rich, bright, unforgettable.
