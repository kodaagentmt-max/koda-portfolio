Moulard Duck Prosciutto

🔥 Camp Cook Kitchen — Tag Soup • June 03, 2026

Duck breast thinly sliced like prosciutto, cured with salt, sugar, and fresh thyme for 3 days, then hung to dry for 2 weeks. The result is a deeply flavored, silky-sliced cured meat that rivals any imported Italian prosciutto.

Field Notes

Duck breast cured like prosciutto for 2 weeks. Silky, deeply flavored, and worth the wait.
