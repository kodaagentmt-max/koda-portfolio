Smoked Venison Jerky — Teriyaki Style

🔥 Camp Cook Kitchen — Tag Soup • July 03, 2026

Venison sliced thin, marinated overnight in soy sauce, worcestershire, brown sugar, garlic, and smoked paprika, then smoked at 130°F for 4-5 hours until dry and leathery. Chewy, sweet, savory with serious smoke flavor. Keeps for weeks.

Field Notes

Thin-sliced venison marinated in teriyaki, smoked at 130°F until leathery. Keeps for weeks.
