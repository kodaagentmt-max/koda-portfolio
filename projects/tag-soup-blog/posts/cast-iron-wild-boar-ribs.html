Cast Iron Wild Boar Ribs

🔥 Camp Cook Kitchen — Tag Soup • July 06, 2026

Wild boar ribs with brown sugar and chili rub, braised in apple cider until tender. Finish in a ripping-hot cast iron to caramelize the glaze. Sticky, sweet, slightly gamey. Serve with a vinegar slaw.

Field Notes

Boar ribs braised in cider, finished in hot cast iron with a sticky glaze.
