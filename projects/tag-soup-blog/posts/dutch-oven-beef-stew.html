Dutch Oven Beef Stew

🔥 Camp Cook Kitchen — Tag Soup • June 28, 2026

Bison or elk chuck cubed and browned, then slow-braised with carrots, potatoes, celery, and onion in beef stock with tomato paste and fresh herbs. The cast iron Dutch oven distributes heat evenly and the stew comes out silky and rich.

Field Notes

Slow-braised wild game stew with root vegetables in a Dutch oven. Rich and silky.
