Cast Iron Venison Chop with Peach Compote

🔥 Camp Cook Kitchen — Tag Soup • July 07, 2026

Thick-cut venison chops seared in a ripping-hot cast iron to medium-rare, rested, then topped with a warm compote of fresh peaches, brown sugar, and a splash of bourbon. The sweet-tart peach against the mineraly venison is an unexpectedly perfect match.

Field Notes
