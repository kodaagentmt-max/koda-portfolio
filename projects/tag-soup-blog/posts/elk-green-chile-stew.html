Elk Green Chile Stew

🔥 Camp Cook Kitchen — Tag Soup • May 1, 2026

Elk shoulder cubed and simmered with fresh Hatch green chiles, potatoes, and onion in a light chicken stock until everything is soft and the elk is pull-apart tender. The chiles bring a bright, vegetal heat that cuts through the richness.

Field Notes

Elk shoulder with Hatch green chiles and potatoes. Bright and warming.
