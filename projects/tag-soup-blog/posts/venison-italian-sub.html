Venison Italian Sub

🔥 Camp Cook Kitchen — Tag Soup • May 11, 2026

Thinly sliced venison roast, provolone, pepperoncinis, giardiniera, and a robust red wine vinaigrette on a hoagie roll. Pressed on a flattop until the cheese melts and the bread gets crispy.

Field Notes
