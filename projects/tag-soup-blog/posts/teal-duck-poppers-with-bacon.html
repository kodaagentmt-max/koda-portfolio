Teal Duck Poppers with Bacon

🔥 Camp Cook Kitchen — Tag Soup • May 1, 2026

Whole teal breasts cut into bite-sized pieces, wrapped in thick-cut bacon, skewered and grilled until the bacon crisps and the duck stays medium-rare inside. The tiny teal has an intensely gamey flavor that the bacon anchors perfectly.

Field Notes

Teal duck wrapped in bacon and grilled. Intense flavor in a small package.
