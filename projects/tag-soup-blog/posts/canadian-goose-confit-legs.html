Canadian Goose Confit Legs

🔥 Camp Cook Kitchen — Tag Soup • June 04, 2026

Goose legs confited submerged in their own fat at 200°F for 3 hours until tender. The legs can then be roasted skin-side down for crispy results, or shredded for tacos, pozole, or nachos. The most underrated part of the goose.

Field Notes

Goose legs confited in goose fat, then crisped. The most underrated part of the goose.
