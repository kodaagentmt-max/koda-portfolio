Southern Fried Rabbit

🔥 Camp Cook Kitchen — Tag Soup • June 05, 2026

Whole rabbit cut into pieces, soaked in buttermilk overnight, then dredged in seasoned flour and fried in a cast iron skillet until golden and crispy. Double-dredge for extra crunch. Serve with mashed potatoes drenched in the frying oil and hot honey.

Field Notes

Buttermilk-soaked rabbit, double-dredged and fried crispy in cast iron. A Southern classic.
