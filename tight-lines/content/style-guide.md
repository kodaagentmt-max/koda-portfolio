# Tight Lines Content — Style Guide

## Voice
- Conversational, like a fishing buddy
- Enthusiastic but not hype-y
- Knowledgable without being preachy
- Use emojis sparingly but purposefully

## Format

### Morning Post — Solunar Forecast (8:15 AM publish, WITH image)
```
🎣 Tight Lines Tackle Box — Daily Forecast

📅 [DATE] | Moon: [PHASE] ([ILLUMINATION])
Major Feed: [TIME] – [TIME]
Minor Feed: [TIME] – [TIME]

🌅 Sunrise: [TIME] | 🌆 Sunset: [TIME]

[SEASONAL TIP — 2-3 sentences, actionable]

Tight lines, KC
```

### Midday Post — Engagement Question (12:00 PM publish, NO image)
```
[ENGAGEMENT QUESTION — a real question about gear, technique, or experience]

Drop your answer below 👇

Tight lines, KC
```

### Afternoon Post — Gear/Trends (3:15 PM publish, WITH image)
```
🆕 What's Working This Week:

[GEAR ITEM 1] — 1-2 sentence description + why it's producing
[GEAR ITEM 2] — 1-2 sentence description + why it's producing

⚡ What lure is YOUR go-to right now? Drop it below 👇

Tight lines, KC
```

## Content Rules
- Morning post: informational, forecast-focused, ends with seasonal tip, includes image
- Midday post: engagement question ONLY, no image, conversational
- Afternoon post: conversational gear talk, engagement question at end, includes image
- Do NOT repeat posts from the posted/ folder (check there first)
- Rotate images from photo-library.json — never reuse the same photo consecutively in the same category

## Seasonal Buckets

### Spring (March–May)
- Walleye post-spawn recovery patterns
- Bass staging on transitions/shallow structure
- Trout rivers — hatches, rising fish
- General: warming water = active fish, use slower retrieves

### Summer (June–August)
- Topwater bass morning/evening
- Bluegill/panfish in backwater
- Walleye deep structure midday
- General: fish are active early/late, slow midday

### Fall (Sept–Nov)
- Pre-spawn bass (October)
- Salmon runs in rivers
- Cooling water = big feed strikes
- General: fish pack on calories before winter

### Winter (Dec–Feb)
- Ice fishing setups
- Slow/deep structure
- Tip-ups and jigging
- General: minimal movement, focus on bait presentation
