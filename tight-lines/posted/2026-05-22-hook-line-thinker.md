## Answer (for admin reference)
TRUE — Fish can drown. They need oxygen dissolved in water, not water itself. If water lacks oxygen (too warm, stagnant), or if a fish's gills are damaged, it can suffocate/drown. Also, some fish like sharks can drown if they stop swimming (obligate ram ventilators).
