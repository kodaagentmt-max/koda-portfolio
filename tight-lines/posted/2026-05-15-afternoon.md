🆕 What's Working This Week:

Berkley Ripfish — A clever hybrid that blends the fast-falling punch of a lipless crankbait with the controlled glide and wobble of a traditional hard bait. The flat bottom lets you kill the fall and keep the bait in the strike zone longer — huge for post-spawn bass and walleye working shallow-to-mid transitions right now.

Under-Spin Jigs — The free-spinning willow blade adds flash and vibration while the jig body works your soft plastic or live bait below. Versatile enough for walleye, bass, and panfish — deadsticking through early summer transitions is where these shine.
