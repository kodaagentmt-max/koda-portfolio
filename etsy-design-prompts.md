- Typography: Clean sans-serif (think: Franklin Gothic, Bebas Neue) or hand-lettered style
- Never include: brand names, team logos, copyrighted characters, trademarks, photo references
- Resolution: 300 DPI minimum for print files. Generate at 4500×5400px for standard tee print area.
